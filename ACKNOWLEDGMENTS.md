## Technical Foundation

### Libraries & Frameworks

- **NumPy, SciPy** — Scientific computing backbone
- **Pandas** — Data manipulation and analysis
- **scikit-learn** — Machine learning baselines
- **PyWavelets** — Discrete wavelet transforms
- **Matplotlib, Seaborn** — Visualization
- **Jupyter** — Interactive notebooks
- **PyTorch, TensorFlow** — Deep learning (optional)

### Methods & Algorithms

- **Conformal Prediction** — Vovk et al. (2005), Balasubramanian et al. (2014)
- **Butterworth Filtering** — Classical signal processing (scipy.signal)
- **Welch's Method** — PSD estimation (scipy.signal)
- **Discrete Wavelet Transform** — Daubechies, Symlets, etc.
